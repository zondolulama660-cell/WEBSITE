Wellness Tips for Students

Student life can be challenging. These wellness tips can help you maintain a healthy and balanced lifestyle.

Manage Academic Stress

Create a study timetable, avoid procrastination, and break large tasks into smaller manageable goals.

Get Enough Sleep

Sleeping for at least 7-8 hours improves concentration, memory, and emotional wellbeing.

Stay Physically Active

Exercise regularly to reduce stress, improve mood, and maintain physical health.

Eat Healthy Meals

A balanced diet helps improve energy levels, focus, and overall wellness.

Talk to Someone

Reach out to friends, family, lecturers, or counselling services when feeling overwhelmed.

Take Study Breaks

Short breaks during study sessions can improve productivity and reduce burnout.

Simple Self-Care Activities

  • Listen to music
  • Go for a walk
  • Practice deep breathing
  • Spend time with friends
  • Take breaks from social media
